## Authority Tier
Tier 3: Mandatory Administrative Form (HM Land Registry)

## Currency Warning
Increasingly replaced by the "Digital Registration Service" (DRS) on the HMLR portal for professional users. Paper/PDF AP1s are becoming the exception for solicitors.

## Template Type
Application to Change the Register

## Structural Skeleton
- **Panel 1:** Local Authority and Postcode.
- **Panel 2:** Title Number(s).
- **Panel 3:** Scope (Whole or Part of Title).
- **Panel 4:** Applications in Priority Order (e.g., Discharge, Transfer, Charge) and Fees.
- **Panel 5:** List of Documents Lodged (TR1, ID1, SDLT5).
- **Panel 6:** The Applicant (New Owner).
- **Panel 7:** The Sender (Solicitor/Conveyancer).
- **Panel 8-10:** Address for Service and Trust Details.
- **Panel 12-14:** Identity Verification (ID1/ID2 requirements).
- **Panel 15:** Signature.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Priority:** The order in Panel 4 is vital; the mortgage discharge must be registered before the new transfer.
- **Fees:** Use the HMLR Fee Calculator; incorrect fees lead to immediate rejection ("Cancellation").
- **ID Verification:** Ensure Panels 12-14 are completed for any unrepresented parties to avoid requisitions.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"I apply to change the register as set out in panel 4... The documents listed in panel 5 are lodged with this application."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The "cover sheet" for all Land Registry submissions. Small firms must ensure the "Sender" details (Panel 7) include a valid email and reference to handle "Requisitions" (requests for more information) efficiently.
