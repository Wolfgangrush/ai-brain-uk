## Authority Tier
Tier 1 — CPR Part 54 Compliance

## Currency Warning
Must be filed "promptly" and in any event within 3 months. Planning cases have a stricter 6-week limit (N461PC).

## Template Type
Judicial Review — Claim Form (N461)

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Details of Parties**: Claimant, Defendant, Interested Parties.
2. **Details of Decision**: Date of decision to be reviewed.
3. **Permission**: Statement of why permission should be granted.
4. **Detailed Grounds**: Reference to attached Grounds of Claim.
5. **Remedy Sought**: Quashing order, mandatory order, injunction, declaration.
6. **Statement of Truth**: Signed by Claimant or Solicitor.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Urgency**: Use Form N463 if interim relief or an urgent hearing is needed.
- **Service**: Must serve the Defendant and Interested Parties within 7 days of filing.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"A claim for judicial review means a claim to review the lawfulness of a decision, action or failure to act in relation to the exercise of a public function." — CPR 54.1(2).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The gateway form for challenging public body decisions. Requires precision in identifying the correct Defendant.
