## Authority Tier
Court Rule Form (CPR Part 42)

## Currency Warning
The notice is not effective until it is both filed with the court and served on all other parties (and the former solicitor).

## Template Type
Court Form / Notice

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Header:** Court, Claim Number, Parties.
2. **The Change:**
   - "The [Claimant/Defendant] [Name] has changed solicitors."
   - "The new solicitors are: [Firm Name and Address]."
   - "The address for service is: [New Address]."
3. **Reference:** New solicitor's reference number.
4. **Signature:** Signed by the new solicitor.
5. **Certificate of Service:** Page 2 to be filled in after service.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Address for Service:** Must be an address within the UK.
- **N434 vs N432:** N434 is for changing solicitor; N432 is a Notice of Funding of costs.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
"TAKE NOTICE that [New Solicitor] has been appointed to act as solicitor for the [Party] in these proceedings in place of [Former Solicitor]."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Used when a solo practitioner takes over a case from another firm or when a client moves from a large firm to a solo advocate.
