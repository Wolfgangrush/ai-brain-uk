## Authority Tier
Court Rule Form (CPR Practice Direction 4)

## Currency Warning
Check for latest court fee changes in the EX50 leaflet. Money claims for a fixed amount should generally be sent to the Civil National Business Centre (CNBC) in Northampton.

## Template Type
Pleading / Court Form

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Header:** Claim Number, Fee Account Number, Help with Fees ref (if any).
2. **Parties:**
   - Claimant's name and address for service.
   - Defendant's name and address.
3. **Brief Details of Claim:** Short summary of the cause of action.
4. **Value:** Amount claimed or range of value (for unspecified claims).
5. **Amount Claimed:** Break down of debt, interest, court fee, and legal representative's costs.
6. **Particulars of Claim:** (Box to include or "Attached" marker).
7. **Statement of Truth:** Mandatory wording and signature.
8. **Defendant's Details:** Name and address for service (bottom of page 2).

## Drafting Conventions
- **Money Claim Online (MCOL):** For simple debts under £100k, MCOL is often the preferred route rather than the paper N1.
- **Statement of Truth:** Must be signed by the claimant or their legal representative. If a representative signs, they must have the claimant's authority.
- **Interest:** Must specify if under contract or s.69 County Courts Act 1984 / s.35A Senior Courts Act 1981.

The standard starting point for initiating any civil claim in the County Court or High Court.
