## Authority Tier
Insolvency Rule Form (Insolvency Rules 2016)

## Currency Warning
The debt must be at least £5,000. It is a class action; you may not get paid if there are many other creditors.

## Template Type
Insolvency Pleading (Form 6.7)

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Parties:** Creditor and Debtor details.
2. **The Debt:** Must be liquidated (fixed) and unsecured.
3. **Statutory Demand:** Details of the demand served at least 21 days prior (and not set aside).
4. **Statement of Truth:** Verified by the creditor.
5. **Hearing Date:** Fixed by the court.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Process:** Statutory Demand -> Bankruptcy Petition -> Bankruptcy Order.
- **Deposit:** A substantial "Official Receiver's Deposit" must be paid.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The debt is a liquidated sum payable immediately and the debtor appears to be unable to pay it."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The "ultimate threat." Often used to force a debtor to the negotiating table. High cost and high risk, but very effective against individuals with significant assets.
