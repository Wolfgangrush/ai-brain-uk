## Authority Tier
Tier 2 — Practice Direction Compliance (CPR PD 29)

## Currency Warning
Local court variations apply. Check if a standard Case Management Information Sheet (CMIS) is required instead of or in addition to a skeleton.

## Template Type
Skeleton Argument — Case Management Conference (CMC)

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Parties & Representatives**: Who is attending.
2. **Current Status**: Pleadings, disclosure, etc.
3. **Directions Sought**: Track, trial window, expert evidence.
4. **Disputed Directions**: Where the parties cannot agree on the timetable.
5. **Costs Budgeting**: Brief reference to Precedent H and any major disputes.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Brevity**: Rarely exceeds 5 pages.
- **Focus**: Procedural efficiency over substantive law.
- **Draft Order**: Always attach the proposed directions order.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The court will expect the parties to have discussed the directions they seek and to have narrowed the issues as far as possible before the CMC." — PD 29.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Ensures the advocate is prepared to argue for a realistic timetable that suits a smaller firm's capacity.
