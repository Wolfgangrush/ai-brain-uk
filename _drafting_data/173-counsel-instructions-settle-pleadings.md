## Authority Tier
Tier 3 — Professional Practice

## Currency Warning
Strict time limits for filing Defence (14/28 days). Ensure Counsel has sufficient "turnaround" time.

## Template Type
Counsel Instructions — Settle Pleadings

## Structural Skeleton
1. **The Claim/Defence**: Nature of the pleading to be drafted.
2. **Key Allegations/Facts**: Specific details to be included or rebutted.
3. **Documents**: Contracts, invoices, or correspondence.
4. **Counsel's Task**: Draft Particulars of Claim, Defence, or Reply.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Deadline**: Highlight the filing deadline clearly.
- **Information**: Ensure Counsel has the "Statement of Truth" facts ready.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"Pleadings must be concise and set out the facts on which the party relies." — CPR 16.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Ensures technical legal requirements are met in complex pleadings, reducing risk of strike-out.
