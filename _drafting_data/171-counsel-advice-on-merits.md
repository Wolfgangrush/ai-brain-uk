## Authority Tier

## Currency Warning
Duty to advise on settlement/mediation. Merit assessments are snapshots and change as evidence emerges.

## Template Type
Counsel Instructions — Advice on Merits

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Purpose**: Specific questions for Counsel (e.g., "What are the prospects of success?").
2. **Material Facts**: Key facts relevant to the legal issues.
3. **Key Documents**: Enclosed documents for review.
4. **Specific Concerns**: Areas where the solicitor is worried (e.g., witness credibility).
5. **Deadline**: When the advice is needed.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Objective**: Ask for an honest "percentage" assessment if possible.
- **Enclosures**: Provide a core bundle to keep costs down.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"Counsel's advice should be objective, independent and focused on the client's best interests."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Validates a solo practitioner's strategy and provides a "second opinion" for the client.
