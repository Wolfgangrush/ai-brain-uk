## Authority Tier
Tier 1 — Insolvency Rules 2016 (Rule 7.5)

## Currency Warning
Requires a court fee (£302) and an insolvency deposit (£2,600). Strictly check Share Capital for court jurisdiction.

## Template Type
Insolvency — Winding-Up Petition

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Court Details**: High Court or County Court.
2. **Petitioner Details**: Name and interest in the company.
3. **Company Details**: Name, Registered Office, Share Capital.
4. **Grounds**: Section 122(1)(f) Insolvency Act 1986 (Unable to pay debts).
5. **The Debt**: Details of the Statutory Demand or unpaid judgment.
6. **Prayer**: Request for the company to be wound up and for costs.
7. **Statement of Truth**: Verification of the petition.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Accuracy**: Errors in company names or addresses can be fatal to the petition.
- **Service**: Must be served at the registered office of the company.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The company is unable to pay its debts as they fall due... it is just and equitable that the company should be wound up." — Form 4.2 (Legacy).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The "nuclear option" for debt collection against a company. Must be used with caution to avoid "abuse of process" claims.
