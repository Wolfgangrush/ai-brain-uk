## Authority Tier
Court Rule (CPR Part 32 & Practice Direction 32)

## Currency Warning
Witness statements for trials in the Business and Property Courts must also comply with PD 57AC, which is significantly more restrictive.

## Template Type
Affidavit / Pleading

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Header (Top Right):** Party, Witness Name, Number of Statement, Exhibit Marks, Date.
2. **Heading:** Court Name, Claim Number, Parties.
3. **Introduction:** "I, [Full Name], of [Address], [Occupation], WILL SAY as follows:"
4. **Relationship:** State if a party, employee of a party, or independent witness.
5. **Knowledge vs Belief:** "1. The facts in this statement are within my own knowledge except where I state otherwise. Where I state facts that are not within my own knowledge, I have stated the source of my information or the basis of my belief."
6. **Preparation:** State how the statement was prepared (e.g., face-to-face meeting).
7. **Body:** Numbered paragraphs in chronological order.
8. **Exhibits:** "There is now produced and shown to me a bundle of documents marked '[Initials]1'..."
9. **Conclusion & Statement of Truth:** Mandatory wording.

## Drafting Conventions
- **First Person:** Must use "I".
- **Own Words:** Avoid "legalese"; the statement must reflect the witness's own language.
- **Statement of Truth:** Essential for admissibility.
- **PD 57AC (BPC only):** Requires a "List of Documents" and an additional confirmation of compliance.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
"I believe that the facts stated in this witness statement are true. I understand that proceedings for contempt of court may be brought against anyone who makes, or causes to be made, a false statement in a document verified by a statement of truth without an honest belief in its truth."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The primary vehicle for presenting factual evidence to the court in civil proceedings.
