## Authority Tier

## Currency Warning
Can be via Zoom/Teams or in-person. Fee should be agreed for "Conference" time.

## Template Type
Counsel Instructions — Conference

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Objective**: Why the conference is being held (e.g., witness proofing, settlement).
2. **Agenda**: List of items to be discussed.
3. **Attendees**: Solicitor, Client, Witnesses, Counsel.
4. **Logistics**: Time, Location/Link.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Preparation**: Send a "Conference Note" with questions in advance.
- **Role**: Solicitor to take detailed minutes.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"A conference with Counsel is an opportunity to clarify strategy and ensure the client understands the risks."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Manages client expectations and builds a cohesive team for the trial.
