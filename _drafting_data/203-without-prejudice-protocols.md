## Currency Warning

## Structural Skeleton
1. **The WP Label**: When and how to apply the label to correspondence.
2. **Admissibility**: Exceptions (e.g., proving a settlement, fraud).
3. **WP Save as to Costs**: Using the label for Part 36 or other settlement offers.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Consistency**: Label every page of a WP letter.
- **Separation**: Do not mix WP and "open" content in the same letter.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The without prejudice rule generally prevents statements made in a genuine attempt to settle a dispute from being put before the court."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Fundamental for safe settlement negotiations. Prevents "admissions" from being used as evidence at trial.
