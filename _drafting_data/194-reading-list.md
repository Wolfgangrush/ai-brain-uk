## Authority Tier
Tier 2 — Court Guide Requirement (BPC)

## Currency Warning
Provide realistic time estimates. Over-estimating wastes court time; under-estimating risks the Judge being unprepared.

## Template Type
Trial Documentation — Reading List

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Essential Reading**: Statements of case, key witness statements, expert reports.
2. **Specific Documents**: High-value exhibits (contracts, key emails).
3. **Time Estimate**: Total hours required for the Judge to pre-read.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Priority**: List documents in order of importance.
- **Location**: Use bundle page references throughout.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The reading list should identify the documents which the judge should read before the trial begins." — Chancery Guide.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Critical for ensuring the Judge starts the trial with a proper understanding of the core issues.
