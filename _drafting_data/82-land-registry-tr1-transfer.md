## Authority Tier
Tier 3: Mandatory Statutory Instrument (Land Registration Rules 2003)

## Currency Warning
Must be signed as a Deed. Electronic signatures (Mercury or witnessed electronic) are now accepted by HM Land Registry if specific protocols are followed.

## Template Type
Transfer of Whole of Registered Title

## Structural Skeleton
- **Panel 1:** Title Number(s).
- **Panel 2:** Property Address.
- **Panel 3:** Date (Completion Date).
- **Panel 4:** Transferor (Seller).
- **Panel 5:** Transferee (Buyer).
- **Panel 6:** Transferee's Address for Service.
- **Panel 7:** The Transfer (Legal Operative Words).
- **Panel 8:** Consideration (Money/Gift/Other).
- **Panel 9:** Title Guarantee (Full/Limited/None).
- **Panel 10:** Declaration of Trust (Joint Tenants/Tenants in Common).
- **Panel 11:** Additional Provisions (Covenants/Easements).
- **Panel 12:** Execution (Signature as a Deed).

## Drafting Conventions
- **Identity Matching:** Names must exactly match the Title Register (Panel 4) and the Passports/ID provided (Panel 5).
- **Joint Ownership:** Panel 10 is critical for tax and inheritance planning; ensure the client's choice is clearly marked.
- **Execution:** Witnesses must be independent (not a relative or party to the deed).

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The transferor transfers the property to the transferee... The transferor gives with [full][limited] title guarantee."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The primary document for any property sale or gift. For a small law firm, accuracy in Panel 10 (Trust) and Panel 11 (Covenants) is the most frequent source of post-completion disputes.
