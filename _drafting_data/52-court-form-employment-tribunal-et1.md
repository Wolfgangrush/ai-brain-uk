## Authority Tier
Tribunal Form (Employment Tribunal Rules of Procedure)

## Currency Warning
Since July 2017, there are no fees for bringing an Employment Tribunal claim. Ensure the ACAS Early Conciliation certificate number is included.

## Template Type
Court Form / Pleading

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Parties:** Claimant and Respondent(s).
2. **ACAS Certificate Number:** (Mandatory).
3. **Employment Details:** Start/end dates, job title.
4. **Pay & Benefits:** Gross/Net pay and frequency.
5. **Section 8 (The Claim):**
   - Type of claim (Unfair dismissal, Discrimination, Redundancy, etc.).
   - Particulars of claim (The factual narrative).
6. **Remedy:** Reinstatement, re-engagement, or compensation.
7. **Representative Details.**

## Drafting Conventions
- **Section 8:** Should be drafted with the same rigor as Particulars of Claim in the County Court, clearly identifying the legal basis (e.g., s.94 ERA 1996 for Unfair Dismissal).
- **Time Limits:** Most ET claims must be brought within 3 months (less one day) of the act complained of.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
"You must have an ACAS Early Conciliation certificate number to make a claim to an employment tribunal, unless an exemption applies."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The standard form for initiating employment law disputes in the UK.
