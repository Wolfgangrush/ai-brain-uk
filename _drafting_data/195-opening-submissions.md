## Authority Tier
Tier 2 — Trial Convention

## Currency Warning
Oral openings are often limited by time. Written submissions must be lodged in advance.

## Template Type
Trial Documentation — Opening Submissions

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Introduction**: The "theory of the case" in a nutshell.
2. **Issues**: What the court must decide.
3. **The Evidence**: What the witnesses will prove.
4. **Legal Test**: Application of law to the anticipated facts.
5. **Conclusion**: Why the claim/defence should succeed.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Persuasiveness**: Use the opening to set the narrative.
- **Brevity**: Avoid repeating the skeleton argument verbatim.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The opening speech is an opportunity to highlight the key themes of the case."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Sets the stage for the trial and helps focus the Judge's attention on the winning points.
