## Authority Tier
Statutory Model (Companies Act 2006 / Model Articles Regulations 2008)

## Currency Warning
The 2008 Model Articles remain the default for all private companies limited by shares incorporated post-Oct 2009 unless amended.

## Template Type
Contract / Constitution

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Introduction:** Definitions and interpretation.
2. **Directors:**
   - Powers and responsibilities.
   - Decision-making (meetings, quorum, written resolutions).
   - Appointment and retirement.
3. **Shares and Distributions:**
   - Issue of shares (Model Art 21).
   - Dividends and other distributions.
4. **Capitalisation of Profits.**
5. **Decision-making by Shareholders:**
   - General meetings (notice, quorum, voting).
   - Written resolutions.
6. **Administrative Arrangements:**
   - Means of communication.
   - Company seal (optional).
   - Directors' indemnity and insurance.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Amended Model Articles:** Most small firms draft "Model Articles with Amendments" to include specific provisions for share transfers (Pre-emption rights), drag-along/tag-along rights, and deadlocks.
- **Shareholders' Agreement:** Often used in tandem with the Articles to handle confidential commercial arrangements between owners.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
