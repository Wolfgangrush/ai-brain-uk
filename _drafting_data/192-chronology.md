## Template Type

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Date**: The date of the event.
2. **Event**: Brief description.
3. **Reference**: [Bundle Page / Document ID].
4. **Significance** (Optional): Why this event matters to the issues.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Neutrality**: Ideally agreed between parties; otherwise, mark disputed events.
- **Formatting**: Three columns (Date, Event, Ref).

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"A chronology is an essential tool for the court to understand the sequence of events without searching the bundle."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Provides a quick-reference guide that helps the Judge follow the evidence during the hearing.
