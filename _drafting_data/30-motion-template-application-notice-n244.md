## Authority Tier
Court Rule Form (CPR Practice Direction 4 / Part 23)

## Currency Warning
Ensure the correct fee is paid; applications "without notice" or "by consent" have different fee levels than "on notice."

## Template Type
Motion / Court Form

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Case Details:** Court, Claim Number, Parties.
2. **Q1-2:** Name of applicant and capacity (Claimant/Defendant).
3. **Q3:** Order sought (the "Prayer").
4. **Q4:** Draft Order attached? (Recommended).
5. **Q5-9:** Hearing details (Type, Time Estimate, Judge level).
6. **Q10:** Evidence relied upon (Witness Statement / Statement of Case / Evidence in the box).
7. **Evidence Box:** (If not using a separate witness statement).
8. **Statement of Truth:** Mandatory wording and signature.

## Drafting Conventions
- **The Prayer (Q3):** Must be precise (e.g., "The Defendant applies for an order that the Claim be struck out pursuant to CPR 3.4(2)(a) on the grounds that it discloses no reasonable grounds for bringing the claim").
- **Draft Order:** Solo advocates should always provide a draft order to assist the District Judge.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
"The [Claimant/Defendant] intends to apply for an order (a copy of which is attached) that: [Specific relief]. Because: [Brief grounds]."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Used for interim applications such as setting aside default judgments, summary judgment, extensions of time, or relief from sanctions.
