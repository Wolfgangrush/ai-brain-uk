## Authority Tier
Tier 3 — Professional Standard

## Currency Warning
Ensure the mediator's liability is limited. Check who is responsible for the venue and refreshments costs.

## Template Type
ADR — Mediation Agreement

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Parties**: Including the Mediator and the Mediation Provider.
2. **Confidentiality**: Strict terms preventing disclosure of mediation discussions.
3. **Without Prejudice**: Ensuring nothing said is admissible in court.
4. **Mediator's Role**: Facilitator, not an advisor or judge.
5. **Termination**: How and when the mediation ends.
6. **Fees**: Allocation of the mediator's fee between parties.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Standard Clauses**: Use the provider's standard agreement if possible.
- **Signatures**: All attendees (including lawyers) should sign.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"All communications made in the course of the mediation are confidential and made on a without prejudice basis."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Protects the client and the solicitor by ensuring the confidentiality of the settlement process.
