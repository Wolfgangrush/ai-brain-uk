## Authority Tier
Court Rule (CPR 40.6)

## Currency Warning
The court has a duty to ensure consent orders are "just"; while most are rubber-stamped, orders involving children or protected parties require judicial scrutiny.

## Template Type
Court Form / Settlement

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Heading:** Court, Claim Number, Parties.
2. **The Order:**
   - "UPON the parties having agreed the terms of this Order:"
   - "BY CONSENT IT IS ORDERED that:"
   - [The substantive terms - e.g., "The Defendant pay the Claimant the sum of £5,000 by 4pm on 1st July 2026"].
   - "2. [Costs provision]."
3. **Signatures:** Signed by both parties' solicitors.

## Drafting Conventions
- **"By Consent":** Must be explicitly stated.
- **Enforcement:** Ensure the order is "final" to allow for enforcement by bailiffs/HCEOs if not complied with.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
"We consent to an order in the above terms. [Signature of Solicitor for Claimant] [Signature of Solicitor for Defendant]."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Used to record any agreement reached between the parties that requires the court's official endorsement (e.g., extensions of time, settlement of the whole claim).
