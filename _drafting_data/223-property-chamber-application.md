## Authority Tier

## Currency Warning
Check if "Section 20" consultation was followed. Renting Homes (Wales) Act 2016 applies in Wales.

## Template Type
Tribunal — Property Chamber Application (Leasehold 3)

## Structural Skeleton
1. **The Property**: Address and details of the lease.
2. **The Parties**: Landlord, Tenant, and Management Company.
3. **The Disputed Charges**: Specific service charge items (e.g., roof repair, insurance).
4. **Grounds for Dispute**: Reasonableness, liability, or failure to consult.
5. **Statutory Reference**: Section 27A Landlord and Tenant Act 1985.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Clarity**: List each disputed year and the specific amount in a table.
- **Fees**: £100 application fee usually applies.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"An application to the Tribunal must be in writing and... state the question which the Tribunal is being asked to decide." — Rule 26.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The primary tool for leaseholders to challenge unfair or excessive service charges.
