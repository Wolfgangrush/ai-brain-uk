## Authority Tier
Tier 1 — Insolvency Rules 2016 Part 8

## Currency Warning
Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA) is an alternative to bankruptcy. Requires a Nominee's report.

## Template Type
Insolvency — IVA Proposal Skeleton

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Debtor's Assets**: Detailed list and valuation.
2. **Debtor's Liabilities**: List of all creditors (secured and unsecured).
3. **The Proposal**: How much will be paid, over what period, and from what funds.
4. **The Nominee**: Details of the licensed insolvency practitioner.
5. **Meeting of Creditors**: Request for a meeting to approve the proposal.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Transparency**: The debtor must make a full and frank disclosure of their affairs.
- **Formatting**: Use the standard IVA protocol if applicable.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The proposal shall set out the terms of the voluntary arrangement which the debtor is proposing to his creditors."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Helps individual clients avoid the stigma and restrictions of bankruptcy while ensuring creditors receive a better return.
