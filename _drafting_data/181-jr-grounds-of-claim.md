## Authority Tier
Tier 1 — CPR Part 54 / PD 54A

## Currency Warning
Focus on legality, not the merits of the decision. Do not treat like a standard appeal.

## Template Type
Judicial Review — Grounds of Claim

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Introduction**: Brief overview of the challenged decision.
2. **Legal Framework**: Statutory context and relevant public law duties.
3. **The Facts**: Essential facts leading to the decision.
4. **Ground 1: Illegality**: Error of law or acting ultra vires.
5. **Ground 2: Irrationality**: Wednesbury unreasonableness.
6. **Ground 3: Procedural Impropriety**: Breach of natural justice or legitimate expectation.
7. **Conclusion**: Why the decision is unlawful.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Numbered Paragraphs**: Essential for referencing.
- **Brevity**: Avoid "rolling" grounds; keep points distinct.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The claim form must include or be accompanied by a statement of the grounds of claim." — CPR 54.6.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The substantive heart of the JR. Correctly categorizing the grounds is vital for the permission stage.
