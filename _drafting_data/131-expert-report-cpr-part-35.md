## Authority Tier
Practice Direction (PD 35 para 3)

## Currency Warning
Mandatory contents are strictly enforced. Missing a Statement of Truth or the "Compliance Statement" can lead to the report being inadmissible.

## Template Type
Expert Evidence

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Qualifications:** Details of the expert's expertise.
2. **Materials:** List of docs/facts relied upon.
3. **Substance:** The analysis and findings.
4. **Summary of Conclusions:** Clear answers to the instructions.
5. **Compliance Statement:** Confirming they understand their duty (PD 35.10).
6. **Statement of Truth:** Specific wording for experts.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Independence:** The report must be the independent product of the expert.
- **Range of Opinion:** If there is a range of opinion on an issue, the expert must summarize it and give reasons for their own view.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"I confirm that I have made clear which facts and matters referred to in this report are within my own knowledge and which are not. Those that are within my own knowledge I confirm to be true."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The document that wins or loses a technical case (e.g., medical negligence, structural failure). Small firms must check the report against PD 35 before filing.
