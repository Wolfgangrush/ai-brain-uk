## Authority Tier
Tier 1 — Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal) Rules 2008

## Currency Warning
Must first apply to the FTT for permission. Only then apply to the UT if FTT refuses.

## Template Type
Tribunal — UT Tax Permission Application

## Structural Skeleton
1. **FTT Decision**: Date and Case Number.
2. **FTT Refusal**: Date of the FTT's refusal of permission.
3. **Errors of Law**: Specific points where the FTT misapplied the law.
4. **Importance of the Issue**: Why the case is significant (if applicable).
5. **Remedy Sought**: Set aside and remake the decision.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Narrow Focus**: The UT only hears "errors of law," not disagreements with facts.
- **OSCOLA**: Use neutral citations for FTT and UT decisions.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"An application to the Upper Tribunal for permission to appeal must be made in writing..." — Rule 21.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The final gateway to overturning a wrong FTT tax decision. Requires high-level legal analysis.
