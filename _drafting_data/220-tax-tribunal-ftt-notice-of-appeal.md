## Authority Tier
Tier 1 — Tribunal Procedure (First-tier Tribunal) (Tax Chamber) Rules 2009

## Currency Warning
Usually 30 days from the date of the HMRC decision letter. Check for "late appeal" provisions.

## Template Type
Tribunal — FTT Tax Notice of Appeal (T240)

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Appellant Details**: Name, Tax Reference, Address.
2. **HMRC Decision**: Date and reference of the decision being appealed.
3. **Grounds of Appeal**: Why the assessment/penalty is wrong.
4. **Relief Sought**: Reduction or cancellation of the tax/penalty.
5. **Enclosures**: Decision letter and any relevant correspondence.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Clarity**: State the specific point of law or fact in dispute (e.g., "reasonable excuse").
- **Track**: Request the appropriate track (Default, Basic, Standard, Complex).

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"A person who wishes to appeal to the Tribunal must give a notice of appeal to the Tribunal." — Rule 20.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Standard form for challenging HMRC tax assessments, VAT decisions, or penalties.
