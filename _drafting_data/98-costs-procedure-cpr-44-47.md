## Structural Skeleton
1. **CPR 44: General Principles**
   - **44.2:** Court's Discretion ("Costs follow the event").
   - **44.3:** Standard vs. Indemnity Basis.
   - **44.3(5):** The Proportionality Test.
2. **CPR 45: Fixed Recoverable Costs (FRC)**
   - **2023/24 Reform:** FRC now applies to almost all claims under £100,000 (Fast Track & Intermediate Track).
   - **Complexity Bands (1-4):** Determines the fixed fee amount.
3. **CPR 46: Special Cases**
   - **46.5:** Litigants in Person (Capped at 2/3 solicitor rate or £19/hr).
   - **46.8:** Wasted Costs Orders (Against the legal representative).
4. **CPR 47: Detailed Assessment**
   - **N252:** Notice of Commencement.
   - **Points of Dispute:** Mandatory 21-day response window.
   - **Provisional Assessment:** For bills under £25,000.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Budgeting (Precedent H):** Mandatory for Multi-Track claims.
- **Summary Assessment (Precedent S):** Required for any hearing lasting less than one day.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The "Money Part" of the case. Small firms must understand CPR 45 (Fixed Costs) to ensure they are charging correctly and CPR 47 to protect against excessive challenges to their bills of costs.
