## Authority Tier
Tier 1 — Tribunal Procedure (First-tier Tribunal) (IAC) Rules 2014

## Currency Warning
14-day deadline if inside the UK; 28 days if outside. High stakes; ensure "Section 120" notice is addressed.

## Template Type
Tribunal — Immigration Tribunal Appeal (IAFT-5)

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Appellant Details**: Home Office reference and personal details.
2. **Decision Details**: Date of Home Office refusal.
3. **Grounds of Appeal**: Protection (Asylum), Human Rights, or EU Settlement Scheme.
4. **Statement of Reasons**: Detailed narrative of why the refusal is unlawful.
5. **Oral Hearing**: Request for a hearing and language interpreter.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Structure**: Align grounds with the specific statutory categories in Section 82 Nationality, Immigration and Asylum Act 2002.
- **Evidence**: Provide a bundle of country information (COI) if relevant.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"A notice of appeal must... set out the grounds of appeal and the reasons for those grounds." — Rule 19.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Standard form for challenging Home Office visa or asylum refusals.
