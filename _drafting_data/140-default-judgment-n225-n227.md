## Authority Tier
Court Rule Form (CPR Part 12)

## Currency Warning
Cannot be used if the defendant has filed an acknowledgment of service or a defense.

## Template Type
Request for Judgment

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Request:** Asking the court to enter judgment because the defendant has failed to respond.
2. **Specified Amount (N225):**
   - Debt amount.
   - Interest to date.
   - Fixed costs.
3. **Unspecified Amount (N227):** Request for "Judgment for an amount to be decided by the court."
4. **Statement of Truth:** Standard wording.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Specified vs Unspecified:** Use N225 for fixed sums (e.g., unpaid invoices) and N227 for damages to be assessed (e.g., personal injury).
- **Service:** Must ensure the time for responding (usually 14 days) has actually expired.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The defendant has not filed an acknowledgment of service or a defense to the claim. I request judgment for the following amount..."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The quickest way to end a case where the defendant is non-responsive. Essential for maintaining cash flow for clients.
