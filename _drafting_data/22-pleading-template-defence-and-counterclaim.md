## Authority Tier
Court Rule (CPR Part 15 & Practice Direction 15)

## Currency Warning
The deadline for filing a Defence is strictly 14 days after service of the Particulars of Claim (or 28 days if an Acknowledgment of Service is filed).

## Template Type
Pleading

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Heading:** Court, Claim Number, Parties.
2. **Admissions:** State which paragraphs of the Particulars of Claim are admitted.
3. **Denials:** State which paragraphs are denied. For every denial, the Defendant must state their reasons and their own version of events (CPR 15.4).
4. **Non-Admissions:** State which paragraphs the Defendant is "unable to admit or deny" but requires the Claimant to prove.
5. **Counterclaim (if any):** Heading "DEFENCE AND COUNTERCLAIM." Set out the cause of action and relief sought by the Defendant against the Claimant.
6. **Limitation:** (If applicable) Plead the Limitation Act 1980 if the claim is time-barred.
7. **Statement of Truth:** Mandatory wording.

## Drafting Conventions
- **CPR 16.5 Compliance:** A simple "blanket denial" is insufficient. Each allegation must be specifically dealt with.
- **Set-off:** If the Defendant is owed money by the Claimant, it should be pleaded as a "defence of set-off."
