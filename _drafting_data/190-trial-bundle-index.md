## Authority Tier
Tier 2 — PD 39A / Court Guides

## Currency Warning
Digital bundles are now the norm (PDF with bookmarks). Hard copies only if requested by the Judge.

## Template Type
Trial Documentation — Trial Bundle Index

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Core Bundle** (if required): Key documents only.
2. **Section A: Statements of Case**: Claim, Defence, Orders.
3. **Section B: Witness Statements**: Chronological or alphabetical.
4. **Section C: Expert Reports**: Including joint statements.
5. **Section D: Chronological Documents**: The main evidentiary documents.
6. **Section E: Legal Authorities**: Sometimes separate.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Pagination**: Consecutive throughout the entire bundle (or section-based if very large).
- **Bookmarks**: Every document in the PDF index must be bookmarked.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The trial bundle must be paginated and indexed, and should only contain documents relevant to the issues." — PD 39A.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The "map" of the trial. A poor index frustrates the Judge and hinders advocacy.
