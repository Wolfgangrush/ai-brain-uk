## Authority Tier
Court Rule Form (CPR Part 72)

## Currency Warning
Only works if the third party (usually a bank) owes the debtor money at the precise moment the order is served.

## Template Type
Enforcement (Garnishee)

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Details of Debt:** Amount remaining unpaid.
2. **The Third Party:** Name and address (e.g., the bank branch).
3. **Account Details:** If known (though not strictly required).
4. **Interim Order:** Directing the bank to "freeze" the amount in the account.
5. **Final Order:** Directing the bank to pay the money to the creditor.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Timing:** Often applied for without notice to prevent the debtor from emptying the account.
- **Costs:** The bank is entitled to a small administrative fee.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The judgment creditor applies for an order that the third party pay to the judgment creditor the amount of any debt due from the third party to the judgment debtor."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Very effective if you know where the debtor banks. It "intercepts" the money before the debtor can touch it.
