## Authority Tier
Tier 1 — Insolvency Act 1986 (Schedule B1)

## Currency Warning
Usually requires an urgent "out of hours" procedure if assets are at risk.

## Template Type
Insolvency — Administration Application

## Structural Skeleton
1. **The Application**: Request for an administration order.
2. **The Company**: Financial status and insolvency test.
3. **The Administrator**: Proposed person and their qualifications/consent.
4. **Purpose**: Achieving one of the statutory objectives of administration.
5. **Evidence**: Witness statement in support (usually from a Director).

## Drafting Conventions
- **Objectives**: Clearly state which objective (e.g., "rescuing the company as a going concern") is being pursued.
- **Service**: Notify the floating charge holder (if any).

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"An administration order is an order appointing a person as the administrator of a company." — Schedule B1 IA 1986.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Used to protect a company from creditor action while a rescue plan is developed.
