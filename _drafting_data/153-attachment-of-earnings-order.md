## Authority Tier
Court Rule Form (Attachment of Earnings Act 1971)

## Currency Warning
Only for individuals who are employees (not self-employed or on benefits).

## Template Type
Enforcement (Salary Deduction)

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Judgment Details:** Unpaid balance.
2. **Debtor's Employment:** Name and address of the employer.
3. **Protected Earnings Rate:** The court will determine the minimum amount the debtor needs to live on.
4. **Payment Frequency:** Weekly or monthly deductions.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Committal:** If the debtor fails to provide employment details, the court can issue a warrant for their arrest (N61).
- **Employer Duty:** The employer is legally required to make the deductions and send them to the court.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"I request an order that the employer of the judgment debtor do make deductions from the debtor's earnings and pay them to the court."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
A "slow and steady" method for enforcing small to medium debts against employed individuals. Provides a reliable stream of payments.
