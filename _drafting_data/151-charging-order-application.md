## Authority Tier
Court Rule Form (CPR Part 73)

## Currency Warning
A charging order secures the debt against property but does not automatically result in payment. You may need an "Order for Sale" later.

## Template Type
Enforcement (Security)

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Details of Judgment:** Court, date, and amount.
2. **Property Details:** Address and Land Registry Title Number.
3. **Ownership:** Confirming the debtor owns the property (solely or jointly).
4. **Other Creditors:** List any known prior mortgages or charges.
5. **Interim Order:** Requested first (without notice).
6. **Final Order:** Requested at a hearing.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Title Search:** Must attach an up-to-date Land Registry search.
- **Joint Property:** If the property is jointly owned, the charge only attaches to the debtor's "beneficial interest."

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The judgment creditor applies for an order imposing a charge on the interest of the judgment debtor in the property described below."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Excellent for securing a large debt where the debtor has no immediate cash but owns a home. Prevents the debtor from selling the house without paying the debt.
