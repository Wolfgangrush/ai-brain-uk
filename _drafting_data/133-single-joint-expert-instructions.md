## Authority Tier
Court Rule (CPR 35.7 - 35.8)

## Currency Warning
Favored by the court for proportionality and cost-saving.

## Template Type
Joint Instructions

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Joint Appointment:** Stating both parties are instructing the expert.
2. **Equal Access:** Ensuring both parties see all communications.
3. **Agreed Facts:** A single set of facts for the expert to work from.
4. **Questions:** Agreed list of questions from both sides.
5. **Fees:** Shared liability for the expert's costs.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Collaboration:** Parties must agree the wording of the instructions.
- **Disputes:** If parties can't agree, the court can settle the instructions.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"We, the Claimant and Defendant, jointly instruct you as a Single Joint Expert to report on the following matters..."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The default for small claims and fast-track cases. It prevents the "battle of the experts" and keeps costs down.
