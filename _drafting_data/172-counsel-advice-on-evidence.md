## Authority Tier
Tier 3 — Professional Standard

## Currency Warning
Must be sought early enough to allow for witness statements or expert reports to be corrected/supplemented.

## Template Type
Counsel Instructions — Advice on Evidence

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Current State of Evidence**: What has been gathered.
2. **Gaps**: Missing documents or witnesses.
3. **Expert Evidence**: Status of reports and areas for cross-examination.
4. **Counsel's View**: What further evidence is needed to prove the case?

## Drafting Conventions
- **Specifics**: Ask Counsel to identify the specific witness or document needed for each issue.
- **Timeline**: Align with the court's directions for witness exchange.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"Advice on evidence is a critical step in trial preparation, identifying the proof required for every material allegation."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Prevents trial collapse due to "missing links" in the evidentiary chain.
