## Authority Tier

## Currency Warning
Ensure compliance with the IR35 (off-payroll working) rules if the service provider is an individual or a personal service company (PSC).

## Template Type
Contract

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Parties:** Consultant/Service Provider and Client.
2. **Services:** Detailed scope of work (Schedule 1).
3. **Fees & Expenses:** Day rates, fixed fees, milestone payments, VAT, and reimbursement policy.
4. **Term & Termination:** Fixed term or rolling; notice period for convenience; immediate termination for material breach.
5. **Intellectual Property (IP):** Clarify who owns the deliverables (usually Client upon payment).
6. **Confidentiality:** Mutual non-disclosure.
7. **Liability & Insurance:** Cap on liability; requirement for Professional Indemnity (PI) insurance.
8. **Status of Provider:** Explicit "independent contractor" status (anti-employment clause).
9. **General:** No partnership, force majeure, governing law.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Deliverables:** Be specific in the Schedule to avoid scope creep and disputes over completion.
- **Substitution:** For IR35 purposes, a right of substitution (where the provider can send someone else) is helpful.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
"The Consultant is an independent contractor and nothing in this Agreement shall render them an employee, worker, agent or partner of the Client and the Consultant shall not hold themselves out as such."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Used when advising freelancers or small service-based businesses on their B2B client contracts.
