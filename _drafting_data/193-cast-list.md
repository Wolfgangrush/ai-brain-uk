## Authority Tier
Tier 2 — Court Guide Convention

## Currency Warning
Include all key individuals mentioned in witness statements, even if not testifying.

## Template Type
Trial Documentation — Cast List

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Name**: Full name and title.
2. **Role**: Party, Employee, Expert, Witness.
3. **Connection**: Brief summary of their involvement in the case.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Alphabetical**: Usually organized by last name.
- **Conciseness**: One or two sentences per person.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The cast list identifies the principal actors in the dispute."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Prevents confusion during oral evidence, especially in cases with many similar names or corporate entities.
