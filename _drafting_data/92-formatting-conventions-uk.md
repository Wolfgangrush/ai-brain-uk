## Authority Tier
Tier 1: Mandatory Procedural Standards (CPR PD 5A, PD 32, PD 57AC; FPR PD 27A; CrimPR 2025)

## Currency Warning
Post-2022 standards prioritize "Digital-First" preparation. Paper conventions remain for fallback and specific tribunal requirements, but electronic bundle (e-bundle) compliance is now the primary metric for professional drafting.

## 1. Physical Document Standards (Fallback/Paper)
### 1.1 Paper & Margins
- **Size:** A4 durable white paper (80gsm+). A3 permitted for large spreadsheets or maps, folded to A4.
- **Margins:** 
  - Left Margin: Minimum 3.5cm (to allow for punching/binding).
  - Other Margins: Minimum 2.5cm (PD 5A para 2.2).
- **Printing:** Single-sided (recto) only, unless explicitly permitted for bulky trial bundles to reduce volume.

### 1.2 Typography & Spacing
- **Font Face:** Times New Roman, Arial, or Calibri. Avoid "serif-heavy" or decorative fonts.
- **Font Size:** 
  - Body Text: 12pt.
  - Headings: 14pt (Bold).
  - Footnotes/Citations: 10pt.
- **Line Spacing:**
  - Pleadings (Particulars of Claim, Defence): 1.5 lines.
  - Witness Statements: 2.0 (Double Spacing) per PD 32 para 19.
  - Affidavits: Double Spacing.
- **Justification:** Left-justified (ragged right) is preferred for accessibility; fully justified text can create "rivers of white" that hinder reading speed.

## 2. Structural Requirements
### 2.1 Numbering
- **Pages:** Numbered consecutively at the bottom center or bottom right.
- **Paragraphs:** Every paragraph must be numbered (1, 2, 3...). Sub-paragraphs should follow the hierarchy: 1.1, 1.1.1, (a), (i).
- **Sections:** Use clear, bolded headings to separate logical phases (e.g., **PARTIES**, **BACKGROUND**, **LIABILITY**, **QUANTUM**).

### 2.2 Statement of Truth
- **Standard Wording (CPR PD 22):** "I believe that the facts stated in this [document] are true. I understand that proceedings for contempt of court may be brought against anyone who makes, or causes to be made, a false statement in a document verified by a statement of truth without an honest belief in its truth."
- **Signatures:** Electronic signatures (typed name or scanned image) are acceptable under the 2020/2022 HMCTS updates, provided the document is filed via a secure portal (CE-File).

## 3. Electronic Bundles (e-Bundles)
Professional drafting is now judged by the quality of the PDF bundle. HMCTS 2022 Standards apply:
- **Format:** PDF/A (archival) or standard PDF.
- **Resolution:** OCR (Optical Character Recognition) is mandatory. Text must be searchable.
- **Index:** The first page must be a hyperlinked Table of Contents.
- **Bookmarks:** Every document and major section must be bookmarked. The bookmark label must match the index entry (e.g., "001 - Claim Form - 2024-01-01").
- **Pagination:** Electronic page numbers must match the PDF page count (i.e., page 1 of the index is page 1 of the PDF).

## 4. Citation Standards
### 4.1 Legal Authorities (OSCOLA)
- **Cases:** *Case Name* [Year] Court Number. (e.g., *Smith v Jones* [2023] EWHC 123 (Comm)).
- **Neutral Citation:** Always use the neutral citation first, followed by the law report (e.g., [2023] UKSC 5, [2023] 1 WLR 45).
- **Statutes:** Short Title Year (e.g., Companies Act 2006). Do not use "The" before the title.

### 4.2 Court-Specific Deviations
- **Commercial Court:** Requires "Composite Schedules" for complex quantum. Position statements limited to 15 pages.
- **Chancery Guide:** Strict rules on "Skeleton Arguments" being served by 10 AM the day before.
- **King's Bench:** Focus on "Master's Directions" for interim applications.
- **TCC (Technology & Construction):** Technical appendices must be high-resolution PDFs with zoom capability.

## 5. Filing Protocols & Naming
### 5.1 File Naming Convention
To ensure clarity in CE-File and DCS (Digital Case System), use the following pattern:
- `<CLAIM_NUMBER>_<DOCUMENT_TYPE>_<PARTY>_<DATE_ISO>.pdf`
- *Example:* `CL-2024-000123_Defence_Respondent_2024-05-28.pdf`

### 5.2 System Gateways
- **CE-File:** High Court (KB, Chancery, Commercial) and Court of Appeal.
- **Money Claim Online (MCMC):** Specified money claims under £100k.
- **Damages Claim Portal (DCP):** Personal injury and general damages.
- **DCS:** All Crown Court criminal matters.

## 6. Practical Tips for Small Firms
- **Styles:** Use MS Word "Styles" for automatic paragraph numbering and TOC generation. Manual numbering is the #1 cause of drafting errors.
- **Redaction:** Use professional redaction tools. "Blacking out" text with a shape tool is not secure and can be reversed in the PDF.
- **File Size:** Keep individual files under 50MB for portal compatibility. Use PDF optimizers to compress images without losing OCR quality.
- **Hyperlinks:** Cross-reference within the draft (e.g., "See paragraph 14 above") using hyperlinked bookmarks to assist the Judge's navigation.

## 7. Trial Bundles (PD 57AC)
For witness statements in the Business and Property Courts:
- **Confirmation:** The witness must sign a "Certificate of Compliance" confirming the statement is in their own words.
- **Drafting:** The solicitor must not "over-lawyer" the statement; it must reflect the witness's actual recollection.
- **List of Documents:** A list of documents referred to in the statement must be provided at the end.

## 8. Summary Checklist
- [ ] A4, 1.5/2.0 spacing, 12pt TNR/Arial.
- [ ] Every page and paragraph numbered.
- [ ] Statement of Truth included with correct 2020 wording.
- [ ] PDF is OCR searchable and bookmarked.
- [ ] OSCOLA/Neutral citation used throughout.
- [ ] File named according to standard convention.
- [ ] Metadata scrubbed (remove "Track Changes" and "Comments").
