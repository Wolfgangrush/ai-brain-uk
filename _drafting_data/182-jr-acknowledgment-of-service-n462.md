## Authority Tier
Tier 1 — CPR Part 54 Compliance

## Currency Warning
Strict 21-day deadline from service of the Claim Form. Failure to file prevents taking part in the permission stage.

## Template Type
Judicial Review — Acknowledgment of Service (N462)

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Tick Boxes**: Do you intend to contest? Is permission opposed?
2. **Summary Grounds of Resistance**: Attached statement.
3. **Interested Parties**: Identify any others who should be notified.
4. **Remedy**: Do you agree with the remedy sought?

## Drafting Conventions
- **Deadline**: Cannot be extended by agreement between parties.
- **Costs**: Include a claim for costs of preparing the AoS if permission is refused.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"Any person served with the claim form who wishes to contest the claim or support it on additional grounds must file an acknowledgment of service." — CPR 54.8.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Primary tool for a Defendant to "knock out" a JR claim at the permission stage.
