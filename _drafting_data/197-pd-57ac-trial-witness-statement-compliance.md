## Authority Tier
Tier 1 — PD 57AC Mandatory Compliance (BPC)

## Currency Warning
Mandatory for Business and Property Courts trial witness statements. Non-compliance results in strike-out or rewriting.

## Template Type
Trial Documentation — PD 57AC Compliance Certificate

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Certificate of Compliance**: Signed by the Legal Representative.
2. **Witness Confirmation**: Signed by the Witness (paragraph 4.1 wording).
3. **Statement of Best Practice**: Reference to the Appendix.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Exact Wording**: Use the verbatim text from PD 57AC.
- **Signature**: Must be signed by the person responsible for the witness.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"I believe this trial witness statement complies with Practice Direction 57AC... and that it has been prepared in accordance with the Statement of Best Practice." — PD 57AC 4.3.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Absolute requirement in the BPC. Failing to include this will lead to immediate procedural failure.
