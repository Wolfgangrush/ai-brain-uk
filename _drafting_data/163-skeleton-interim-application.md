## Authority Tier
Tier 2 — Practice Direction Compliance (PD 23A)

## Currency Warning
Strict page limits (often 10 pages) apply in the Commercial and Chancery Courts for interim applications.

## Template Type
Skeleton Argument — Interim Application

## Structural Skeleton
1. **The Application**: What is being sought (e.g., set aside, disclosure).
2. **Test to be Applied**: The legal standard (e.g., "real prospect of success").
3. **The Evidence**: Summary of witness statements in support/opposition.
4. **Arguments**: Analysis of how the facts meet the legal test.
5. **Conclusion**: Order requested.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Page Limit**: Keep under 10 pages unless complex.
- **Timing**: Must be filed and served at least 2 days before the hearing for short applications.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The skeleton argument should identify the documents in the bundle that the judge needs to read, including any witness statements." — PD 23A.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Critical for securing interim relief where timing and clarity are paramount.
