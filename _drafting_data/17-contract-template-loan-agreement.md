## Currency Warning
If the lender is in the business of lending to individuals, they may require authorization from the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) under the Consumer Credit Act 1974. This template is for unregulated B2B or private loans.

## Template Type
Contract

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Parties:** Lender and Borrower.
2. **The Loan:** Principal amount.
3. **Interest:** Rate (fixed/floating) and calculation method.
4. **Repayment:** Date of repayment or instalment schedule.
5. **Prepayment:** Right to pay early (with or without penalty).
6. **Representations & Warranties:** Borrower's authority to borrow; no existing defaults.
7. **Events of Default:** Non-payment, insolvency, cross-default.
8. **Remedies:** Acceleration of debt; right to charge default interest.
9. **Boilerplate:** No waiver, whole agreement, governing law.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Default Interest:** Should not be so high as to constitute a "penalty" at common law.
- **Security:** If the loan is secured, a separate Charge or Mortgage document is required.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
"The Borrower shall pay interest on the Loan at the rate of [X]% per annum. Such interest shall accrue on a daily basis and be payable in arrears on the Repayment Date."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Commonly used for director loans to a company, inter-company lending, or private loans between individuals.
