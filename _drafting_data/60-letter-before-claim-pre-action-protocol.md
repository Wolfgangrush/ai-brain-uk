## Authority Tier
Court Rule (Pre-Action Protocol for Debt Claims)

## Currency Warning
The Debt Protocol applies only to B2C or B2Sole-Trader debts. Business-to-Business (B2B) debts are governed by the general Practice Direction on Pre-Action Conduct.

## Template Type
Letter Before Action

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Date & Method of Sending:** (Usually post).
2. **The Amount:** Total debt, breakdown of interest and charges.
3. **Basis of Debt:** Reference to contract (date, parties) or oral agreement details.
4. **Assignment:** (If applicable).
5. **Payment Terms:** How and where to pay.
6. **Enclosures (Mandatory for Debt Protocol):**
   - Information Sheet.
   - Reply Form.
   - Financial Statement Form.
   - Statement of Account.
7. **The 30-Day Rule:** State clearly that the debtor has 30 days to respond before a claim is issued.
8. **ADR:** Offer to discuss or use mediation.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Compliance:** Non-compliance can lead to a stay of proceedings and the creditor being denied their costs even if they win.
- **Standard Forms:** Do not modify the ACAS or HMCTS standardized reply forms enclosed.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
"You must respond to this letter within 30 days. If you do not respond, we may start a court claim against you without further notice."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Essential for debt recovery work to ensure costs are recoverable and proceedings are not delayed.
