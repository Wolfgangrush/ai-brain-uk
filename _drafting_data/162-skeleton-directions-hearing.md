## Currency Warning
Often combined with CMC or CCMC. Verify if the hearing is strictly for directions or has a broader scope.

## Template Type
Skeleton Argument — Directions Hearing

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Objective**: What directions are needed (e.g., stay for ADR, expert instructions).
2. **Compliance**: Brief update on compliance with previous orders.
3. **Proposed Timetable**: New deadlines if current ones are missed.
4. **Costs**: Statement of costs for the hearing.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Conciseness**: 2-3 pages usually suffice.
- **Evidence**: Refer to Application Notice (N244) if applicable.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"At any hearing of an application for directions, the court will seek to ensure that the directions are necessary and proportionate." — CPR 29.2.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Quick reference for minor hearings to ensure no procedural point is missed.
