## Authority Tier
Court Rule (CPR Part 25)

## Currency Warning
High duty of "Full and Frank Disclosure." Any failure to disclose facts helpful to the defendant can result in the injunction being discharged with indemnity costs.

## Template Type
Urgent Interim Application

## Structural Skeleton
1. **N244 Application Notice:** Stating "without notice" and the relief sought.
2. **Draft Order:** Including the "Penal Notice."
3. **Evidence (Witness Statement/Affidavit):**
   - Urgent need for the order.
   - American Cyanamid test (Serious question, Damages inadequate, Balance of convenience).
   - Disclosure of all material facts (including those against the applicant).
4. **Undertaking as to Damages:** Included in the draft order.

## Drafting Conventions
- **American Cyanamid Principles:** The "Bible" for interim injunctions.
- **Status Quo:** The court's default is to maintain the status quo.
- **Service:** Must serve the order as soon as possible after it is granted.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"If you [the Respondent] disobey this order you may be held to be in contempt of court and may be imprisoned, fined or have your assets seized."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Used in "emergency" situations (e.g., stopping the sale of a property, preventing use of trade secrets). High risk due to the cross-undertaking in damages.
