## Authority Tier
Tier 2 — Trial Convention

## Currency Warning
Often ordered to be exchanged simultaneously. Incorporate reactions to live testimony.

## Template Type
Trial Documentation — Written Closing Submissions

## Structural Skeleton
1. **The Evidence as it Transpired**: Summary of what witnesses actually said.
2. **Credibility**: Commenting on the performance of witnesses under cross-examination.
3. **Resolution of Issues**: Applying the law to the *proven* facts.
4. **Quantum**: Final figures if liability is established.
5. **Relief Requested**: Detailed order sought.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Agility**: Must be drafted/refined during the trial.
- **References**: Use the transcript or Judge's notes if available.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"Closing submissions summarize the party's position after all the evidence has been heard."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The final chance to persuade the Judge, especially on disputed facts and witness reliability.
