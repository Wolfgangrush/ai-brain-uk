## Structural Skeleton
1. **Commercial Court Guide:** The "Gold Standard" for high-value business disputes. *Strict page limits and bundle standards*.
2. **Chancery Guide:** Governs trusts, probate, and company law. *Focus on "Skeleton Arguments"*.
3. **King's Bench Guide:** General civil litigation in the High Court.
4. **TCC Guide:** Technology and Construction Court - *Specialist rules for technical evidence*.
5. **Administrative Court Guide:** Essential for Judicial Review and Statutory Appeals.
6. **Employment Tribunal Presidential Guidance:** Standards for ET1/ET3 drafting and bundle preparation.
7. **Small Claims Guide:** Simplified procedures for claims under £10,000.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Guide Precedence:** In specialist courts, the Guide often contains stricter requirements than the general CPR. Always check the Guide first.
- **Templates:** Many Guides (e.g., Commercial Court) contain their own specific templates for Case Management Information Sheets.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Critical for "Leveling Up" when appearing in the High Court. A solo advocate using a standard CPR template in the Commercial Court will be immediately identified as "out of their depth" if they ignore the Commercial Court Guide's specific formatting.
