## Structural Skeleton
1. **Case Management (Parts 1-3)**
   - **PD 1A:** Vulnerable Parties.
   - **PD 3E:** Costs Management (Budgeting).
2. **Documents & Service (Parts 5-6)**
   - **PD 5A:** Formatting of Documents (Master Style Guide).
   - **PD 5B/PD 51R:** Electronic communication and Online Civil Money Claims.
3. **Statements of Case (Parts 15-18)**
   - **PD 16:** Detailed requirements for Particulars of Claim and Defence.
4. **Evidence & Trial (Parts 31-39)**
   - **PD 31B:** Disclosure of Electronic Documents (EDRM).
   - **PD 32:** Evidence (Witness Statements/Affidavits).
   - **PD 35:** Expert Evidence.
5. **Specialist Proceedings (Parts 51-57)**
   - **PD 57AC:** Trial Witness Statements in Business and Property Courts (Strict compliance required).
   - **PD 57AD:** Disclosure Pilot (extended to permanent status).
6. **Appeals & Enforcement (Parts 52-89)**
   - **PD 52A-E:** Appeal pathways.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Pillar PDs:** PD 16, PD 32, and PD 5A are the "Holy Trinity" for any civil litigator.
- **Strict Compliance:** PD 57AC carries heavy sanctions (strike-out) for "over-lawyered" witness statements.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The primary "map" for navigating civil litigation. Use this index to cross-check that a POC (Particulars of Claim) meets every specific requirement of PD 16 before filing.
