## Structural Skeleton
1. **General Protocol:** *Practice Direction – Pre-Action Conduct and Protocols* (Applies where no specific protocol exists).
2. **Debt Claims:** For businesses claiming debt from individuals. *Includes mandatory 30-day response window*.
3. **Personal Injury:** Standard PI, Clinical Disputes, and Disease/Illness.
4. **Professional Negligence:** Mandatory "Letter of Claim" and "Letter of Response" framework.
5. **Construction & Engineering:** *The "Pre-Action Meeting" requirement*.
6. **Property:** Dilapidations, Housing Conditions, and Possession (Mortgage/Social Landlord).
7. **Public Law:** Judicial Review PAP - *Letter before Claim is mandatory*.
8. **Digital Protocols (2025/26):** RTA Small Claims / Official Injury Claim (OIC) portal.

## Drafting Conventions
- **ADR:** Every protocol now emphasizes Alternative Dispute Resolution.
- **Costs Sanctions:** Failing to follow a PAP can result in the winner being denied their costs (CPR 44.2).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Prevents "premature" litigation. For a small firm, the Debt PAP and Professional Negligence PAP are the most common traps—missing a deadline or required document here can be fatal to a future claim.
