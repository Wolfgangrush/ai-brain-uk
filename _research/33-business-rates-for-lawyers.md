# Business Rates and Guideline Hourly Rates (GHR) — UK

## Source URL
- [Guideline Hourly Rates (Judiciary.uk)](https://www.judiciary.uk/guidance-and-resources/guideline-hourly-rates/)

## Last Verified
2026-05-18

## Authority Tier
Official Guidance (Judicial)

## Currency Warning
The GHR are now updated annually on **1 January** using an automatic inflationary uplift (SPPI). The 2026 rates include a 2.28% increase from 2025.

## Key Facts
1. **Grade A (8+ years PQE):** Solicitors/Legal Executives. Rates range from **£288** (National 2) to **£579** (London 1).
2. **Grade B (4+ years PQE):** Solicitors/Legal Executives. Rates range from **£247** (National) to **£393** (London 1).
3. **Grade C (<4 years PQE):** Other solicitors/equivalents. Rates range from **£200** (National) to **£305** (London 1).
4. **Grade D (Trainees/Paralegals):** Rates range from **£142** (National) to **£210** (London 1).
5. **Regional Bands:**
    - **London 1:** Heavy commercial work.
    - **London 2:** City/Central London.
    - **London 3:** Outer London.
    - **National 1:** Major regional centers (Birmingham, Manchester, etc.).
    - **National 2:** Everywhere else.
6. **Usage:** Courts use these as a starting point for "summary assessment" of costs at the end of a hearing.
7. **Market Rates:** Actual market rates for solo advocates can be higher or lower depending on the niche, but the GHR act as a "ceiling" for what can be recovered from the losing side.

## Verbatim Quote (if applicable)
"The guideline rates... may assist judges in the summary assessment of costs... they are not intended to be a 'ready-reckoner'." — *GHR Guidance (Paraphrased)*

## Practical Implication for Solo Advocate
A solo advocate should benchmark their fees against the **GHR**. If they charge significantly more than the GHR, they must warn their client that the "gap" in costs will not be recoverable from the other side even if they win. For "unbundled" services or fixed fees, soloists can often offer rates lower than GHR to remain competitive, especially in National 2 areas.
