# Citation Format (Primary Sources) — UK (OSCOLA)

## Source URL
- [OSCOLA 4th Edition (Oxford Law)](https://www.law.ox.ac.uk/oscola)

## Last Verified
2026-05-18

## Authority Tier
Reputable Secondary (Industry Standard)

## Currency Warning
OSCOLA 4th Edition is the current standard. Note that some courts (e.g., the Supreme Court) have their own slightly modified styles for judgments, but OSCOLA remains the standard for practitioners and academics.

## Key Facts
1. **Neutral Citation (Post-2001):** Cases are cited by name, year, court abbreviation, and judgment number.
    - *Example:* *Corr v IBC Vehicles Ltd* [2008] UKHL 13.
2. **Law Reports Citation:** If a case is reported, the report citation follows the neutral citation.
    - *Example:* *Corr v IBC Vehicles Ltd* [2008] UKHL 13, [2008] 1 AC 884.
3. **Report Hierarchy:** The Law Reports (AC, QB, Ch, Fam) must be cited first, followed by WLR or All ER.
4. **Statutes:** Cited by short title and year, without punctuation.
    - *Example:* Human Rights Act 1998.
5. **Statutory Instruments (SIs):** Cited by name, year, and SI number.
    - *Example:* Penalties for Disorderly Behaviour (Amendment of Minimum Age) Order 2004, SI 2004/3166.
6. **Pinpointing:** To refer to a specific page or paragraph:
    - *Statute:* Human Rights Act 1998, s 15(1).
    - *Case:* *Sumner v Colborne* [2018] EWCA Civ 1006 [21].
7. **Minimal Punctuation:** No full stops in abbreviations (e.g., `AC` not `A.C.`, `r` not `r.`).

## Verbatim Quote (if applicable)
"The primary purpose of legal citation is to enable the reader to find the relevant authority quickly and easily." — *OSCOLA 4th Ed, Introduction*

## Practical Implication for Solo Advocate
Using correct OSCOLA citations in skeleton arguments and letters of claim is a signal of professionalism. Most UK legal databases (Westlaw, LexisNexis, BAILII) provide the neutral citation prominently. A solo advocate's software should automate these formats to ensure compliance with court practice directions that often mandate the use of neutral citations.
